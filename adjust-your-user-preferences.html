Uptempo Documentation Help

Adjust your user preferences

You can adjust various aspects of the user experience in Uptempo to suit your personal preferences.

You can set:

  • Your preferred number format to customize how numbers are formatted based on regional conventions.

  • Your preferred date format to customize how dates are formatted.

  • Your preferred start page, the page in Uptempo that is displayed by default each time you sign in.

All of these settings are specific to your user account, and do not affect other users of your Uptempo instance.

Customize how numbers are formatted in Uptempo

Every country has its own conventions for representing numbers. These include how digits are grouped, which characters are used to separate decimals and digit groups, and so on.

You can customize the regional number format in Uptempo so that numbers are displayed in a way that is familiar and intuitive to you.

Set your preferred regional number format

  1. On any page in Uptempo, click the User Menu (the button with your initials) in the page header.

  2. In the User Menu, click My Profile:

  3. On the Edit Profile page, use the Number Format menu to set your preferred regional number format. Select the country whose standard for representing numbers you want to use in Uptempo.

Your change is saved automatically, and takes effect immediately.

    Customize how dates are formatted in Uptempo

    In calendar dates, the order in which the day, month, and year are typically displayed varies by region.

    You can customize the date format in Uptempo so that dates are displayed in the order you expect, and with your preferred separator character (- or /).

    Set your preferred date format

    1. On any page in Uptempo, click the User Menu (the button with your initials) in the page header.

    2. In the User Menu, click My Profile:

    3. On the Edit Profile page, use the Date Format menu to set your preferred date format. You can choose between the following options:

      YYYY-MM-DD

      Displays June 10, 2026 as: 2026-06-10. The ISO 8601 standard format for calendar dates.

      MM/DD/YYYY

      Displays June 10, 2026 as: 06/10/2026. The format typically used in the United States.

      DD/MM/YYYY

      Displays June 10, 2026 as: 10/06/2026. The format typically used in most countries, with a slash / as the separator.

      DD-MM-YYYY

      Displays June 10, 2026 as: 10/06/2026. The format typically used in most countries, with a hypen - as the separator.

      YYYY/MM/DD

      Displays June 10, 2026 as: 2026-06-10. Similar to the ISO 8601 standard format, but with a slash / as the separator.

    Your change is saved automatically, and takes effect immediately.

      Customize your default start page in Uptempo

      You can customize your start page in Uptempo so that signing in always takes you to the page you use most often.

      Set your Uptempo start page

      1. On any page in Uptempo, click the User Menu (the button with your initials) in the page header.

      2. In the User Menu, click My Profile:

      3. On the Edit Profile page, use Start Page menu to set your preferred starting page after you sign in.

      Your change is saved automatically, and takes effect the next time you sign in to Uptempo.
