Reallocate investment funds

Leaving funds unspent can signal that your budget was too high, putting you at risk for budget cuts next year. To protect your funding and stay in control, proactively track your variance using Forecast status tags and Forecasting.

If you need to shift funds, you can reallocate them in two ways:

  • Within the same investment plan: Reallocate investments

  • Between different investment plans: Reallocate budgets
