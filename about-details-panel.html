About the investment details panel

The investment details panel in the Investments section shows details about the selected investment item. Items can be categories, sub-categories, line items, or placeholders.

Details

Shows basic information and attribute values for the investment.

Metrics

Contains the metrics configured for your investment plans, such as top-down targets and variance between plan and actual spend.

Strategy

Displays objectives defined with Strategic Planner.

Alignment

Shows your alignment score and recalculates as you update your investment plans.

Activities

Lets you connect activities from Uptempo Campaign Management to the investment and manage fund allocation to connected activities.

The sections in the details panel vary depending on which features are enabled in your Uptempo instance.

Open an investment's details panel

  1. Go to the Investments section.

  2. In the Hierarchy column, click Open Panel on the investment category, sub-category, or line item whose details you want to view.

The investment details panel opens.

    Uptempo ID and Persistent ID

    Uptempo ID

    Marketing data typically spans multiple systems, including CRMs, procurement tools, finance ERPs, and digital marketing platforms. This makes it difficult to connect all data points for comprehensive reporting.

    Uptempo Financial Management automatically generates a unique Uptempo ID for each item when it’s created. These IDs are assigned to budget folders, sub-folders, investment plans, and all investment items (categories, sub-categories, placeholders, and line items). You can find an item’s ID on its Roll-Up and details panels. It’s named after the item type, for example Line Item ID or Investments ID.

    You can use an item’s ID at three levels:

    • Budget hierarchy: Connect it to other planning tools.

    • Investment category or sub-category: Tag it to POs with multiple lines, or to parent campaigns with multiple programs or children.

    • Line item: Tag it to individual PO line items, transactions, campaigns, or projects.

    Persistent ID

    When an investment category, sub-category, or line item is copied for the first time, Financial Management also assigns it a Persistent ID that matches the investment item's original Uptempo ID. The Persistent ID provides a consistent reference ID across multiple years, so you only need one ID across systems year over year.

    Whenever you copy an investment item from one year to the next during rollover, the copy receives a new unique Uptempo ID (specific to the new budget year), but retains the same Persistent ID as the original it was copied from. This means that the same Persistent ID links the original investment item to all of its copies. Investment items that share a Persistent ID are still unique, and don't affect each other: changing a value in one line item doesn't affect any other line item with the same Persistent ID.

    In addition to investment items, Uptempo also assigns Persistent IDs to these Financial Management objects:

    • In the Investments section:

      • Categories

      • Sub-categories

      • Line items

    • In the Financial Management settings:

      • Columns

      • Column choices (list options for columns with the types Drop-down list or Multi-select list)
