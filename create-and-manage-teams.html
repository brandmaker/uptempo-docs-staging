Create and manage teams

To apply activity access controls to your Campaign Management users, you can create teams to manage multiple users with the same access needs as a single entity. When you assign a team to an access control policy, the policy's access permissions automatically extend to all users in the team.

Using teams to apply access controls helps you to save time and administrative overhead, and reduces the risk of configuration errors. It also makes ongoing maintenance easier: whenever access needs change due to users joining, leaving, or changing roles, you can easily add users to or remove them from teams to adjust their access, instead of having to modify multiple access control policies.
