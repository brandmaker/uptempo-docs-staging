v15.09.0

Available from January 6, 2026.

Batch Adjust Spend Changes

A user may now adjust all selected Yield Curves proportionally via the Total Performance Estimates table at the top of the screen. By manually entering a Spend amount or Percent Difference in this table, all component Yield Curves will be adjusted by the same amount.

Pin Total PE Section to Top of Screen

Users can also choose to pin this section to the top of their screen by toggling Lock UI to ON.
