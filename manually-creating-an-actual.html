Manually create actuals

You can create actuals manually in Uptempo.

Manually create an actual

  1. In the Investments section, click Open Panel in line with the investment that you would like to add an actual to.

  2. On the details panel, click Actuals to open the Actuals dialog.

  3. Click the Add Actual button.

  4. In the Actuals dialog, fill in the details for the actual. You must enter at least the Amount and Date.

  5. Close the Actuals dialog.

You have manually created an actual.
