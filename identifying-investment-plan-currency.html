Identify the investment plan currency

If your organization operates internationally and the global marketing teams manage their investments in their local currencies, your Uptempo administrator can set the local currently for each regional marketing plan.

You can

Identify an investment plan's currency

  1. Open the Budgets section.

  2. Find the investment plan for which you want to find the currency. The currency of the investment plan is listed under the Currency column.

You can only view an investment plan's currency in the Budgets section. It is not shown when viewing the investment plan in the Investments section.
