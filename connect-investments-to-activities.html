Connect investments to activities

To represent how the activities in your marketing plan are funded, you can connect investments in Uptempo Financial Management to the activities they are intended to fund.

You can:

  • Connect activities at any level of the activity hierarchy to investments (but your administrator may restrict connections to only certain activity types).

  • Connect activities to one or multiple investments, at any level of the investment hierarchy (category, sub-category, or line item).

Connections between activities and investments are governed by a set of rules to prevent connections that would cause funding conflicts or connections that aren't valid. To learn more, see Rules for connecting investments.

Connect an activity to an investment

  1. In the Activities section, find the activity you want to connect to an investment in the activity hierarchy panel. Click on the activity's name to open its details panel.

  2. Click on the Budget tab section in the details panel.

  3. Click Connect to Spend.

  4. In the Select Investment to connect to... dialog, use the Select Budget menus to choose the budget you want to use. The Investment Item list is displayed that shows available investments in the selected budget.

  5. In the Investment Item list, use the checkboxes to select an investment you want to connect. If needed, you can select multiple investments.

  6. Click Connect Investments to connect the selected investments to the activity:

    You can select investment items from more than one budget. When you switch budgets, any investments you have already selected remain selected, and will be connected when you click Connect Investments.

  7. The Select Investment to connect to... dialog closes, and you are returned to the Budget tab section of the activity's details panel.

The selected investments are now connected to the activity, and you can view and track investment data for the activity. For details, see Track and analyze activity budgets.

You can also connect investments while creating a new activity, on the Budget step in the Create Activity setup assistant. The procedure is otherwise the same as the one described here.

    Disconnect an investment from an activity

    Disconnecting an investment from an activity permanently deletes funds allocation amounts associated with the connection from Uptempo Financial Management.

    The deleted data can't be recovered.

    1. In the Activities section, find the activity you want to connect to an investment in the activity hierarchy panel. Click on the activity's name to open its details panel.

    2. Click on the Budget tab section in the details panel.

    3. Click on any of the spend data sections to expand it and view the connected investments.

    4. In the expanded spend data section, click Disconnect on the investment you want to disconnect.

    5. Review the information in the confirmation dialog to ensure you understand what will happen when you disconnect the investment. To finish the disconnection, click Disconnect Activity.

    The investment is disconnected with immediate effect, and is no longer listed on the Budget tab section of the activity's details panel.
