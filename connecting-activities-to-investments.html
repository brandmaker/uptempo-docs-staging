Connect activities to investments

To use an investment item to fund marketing activities, you can connect one or multiple activities to it.

If only one activity is connected to an investment, 100% of its funds are automatically allocated to the activity.

If you connect two or more activities to an investment, you can choose how you want funds allocations to be distributed between these activities. For more information, see Set how funds are distributed to connected activities.

Connect activities to an investment

  1. In Uptempo, click Investments in the navigation to view the investment hierarchy.

  2. In the investment hierarchy, find the investment that you want to connect activities to and click Open Panel. The investment's Details Panel opens.

  3. Click Activities, then click Connect Activity:

  4. In the Select activity to connect to... dialog, select the activity (or activities) you want to connect, then click Connect Activities:

    You can select multiple activities to connect to line items, but you can only connect one activity to categories and sub-categories.

  5. The list of activities closes, and the activity (or activities) you connected are now displayed in the Activities tab of the investment's Details panel:

    • Click Search to view the details of an activity in Uptempo Campaign Management.

    • Click Disconnect to remove an activity from the investment.

You have connected activities to an investment.

    Adjust investment funds allocation

    When you connect multiple activities to an investment, Uptempo will automatically distribute the investment's funds equally between all connected activities. If you want to adjust these allocations, you can use the Activity Funding Panel.

    For instructions, see Change how an investment's funds are allocated
